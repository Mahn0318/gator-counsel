About Us

Gator Counsel was founded in 2022 in Gainesville, FL, USA. We are 4 students from Software Engineering who wanted to create a website where people from all over the world could express their feelings and emotions with other people, to help them get a better mental health.

Main Goal

Gator Counsel is a newly and fresh project focused on the wellness and care of our users. It offers an innovative way to have group counseling or private chats with your psycologist.
