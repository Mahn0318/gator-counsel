How do I create a private chat?

Firstly you have to go to the chats page and select the add option, afterwards you will have the option to add all the information that you desire, in order to express how you experience was and help other users in their future.

How many private chats can I have?

As much as you want.

How many group chats can I have?

As much as you want. We impose no limits on this.

Who is Gator Counsel intended audience?

Gator Counsel is designed to be inclusive of all age ranges, abilities, and budget-levels.

How often is Gator Counsel updated?

Gator Counsel is updated constantly since users are all the time adding new stuff, we also have experts taking care of any errors that may ocurr.

Can I contribute to Gator Counsel?

You can contribute by creating you experiences or by sharing the experiences of others through any of your social media.
